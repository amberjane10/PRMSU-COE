Faculty Directory

College Dean

Engr. Gilbert D. Deboma, REE, M.Eng.

Engr. Gilbert D. Deboma has been a faculty member at the College of Engineering for 24 years, currently holding the academic rank of Associate Professor V. He earned his Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from the Technological Institute of the Philippines – Quezon City. Engr. Deboma furthered his education by obtaining a Master of Science in Electronics Engineering from Andong National University in South Korea. He is currently pursuing a PhD in Agricultural Engineering at Central Luzon State University.

Engr. Deboma is an active member of the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers, Inc., the accredited professional organization of electrical practitioners in the Philippines. He currently serves as the Dean of the College of Engineering, as well as the Program Chairperson for the Electrical Engineering Department.

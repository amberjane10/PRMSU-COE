Instruction - PRMSU College of Engineering

Curricular Offerings

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering program blends electrical engineering and computer science, equipping students with the skills needed to design and develop computer systems, software, and hardware solutions.

Civil Engineering

This program trains students in the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as bridges, roads, and buildings, with a focus on sustainability and safety.

Mining Engineering

The Mining Engineering program focuses on the extraction of natural resources from the earth, covering exploration, processing, and environmental considerations in mining operations.

Electrical Engineering

This program covers the study of electrical systems, power generation, electronics, and communications, preparing students to work in various sectors such as energy, automation, and telecommunication.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering program emphasizes the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems, from engines to robotics, with a focus on innovation and problem-solving.
